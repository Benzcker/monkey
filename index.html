A monkey types at a 26-letter keyboard with one key corresponding to each of the lower-case letters until he randomly writes your word. Each keystroke is chosen independently and uniformly at random from the 26 possibilities.
Notice: You can tell the monkey how many keystrokes he should do per second, but how many he really does depends on the strength of your PC.
A word with more than 3 letters can take hours to find! Just start with some short words like "hi".
